About Priyadarshini College

Priyadarshini Institute of Science and Technology for Women, one more feather in the crown of the Priyadarshini Educational Group, Sponsored By J.K. Education Society, has commenced its maiden voyage from the year 2009-2010 with a crew to work for the upliftment of women in the world arena. tsnpds

Our Facilities

A comfortable and straightforward accommodation offering both A/C and non-A/C rooms.

A strong focus on enhancing employment opportunities and promoting research activities.

Students at PRIW enjoy a vibrant social life, filled with cultural, sports, and socially driven events.

Surrounded by lush greenery, students breathe fresh air that calms the mind and inspires fresh ideas.

Pristine, eco-friendly, and vibrant, the campus offers an ideal environment for learning.

Our faculty are not only experts in their fields but also supportive, engaging, enthusiastic, and caring.
